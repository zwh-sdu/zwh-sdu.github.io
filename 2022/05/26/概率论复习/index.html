P[A∣B]=P[A∩B]P[B]P[A|B]=\frac{P[A\cap B]}{P[B]} P[A∣B]=P[B]P[A∩B]​

This conditional probability P[A∩B]P[A\cap B]P[A∩B] is read:

  • “The conditional probability of A conditioned on B” or simply
  • “The probability of A given B”

The Central Limit Theorem 中心极限定理

Why Gaussian

  • Analytical tractability 分析易处理

    • (μ,Σ)(\mu,\Sigma)(μ,Σ) are sufficient to uniquely characterize the distribution.

      (μ,Σ)(\mu,\Sigma)(μ,Σ) 足以唯一描述分布

    • If (Gaussian) xix_ixi​’s are mutually uncorrelated, then they are independent.

      如果变量之间互不相关，则它们也互相独立。

    • The marginal and conditional densities are also Gaussian.

      边缘分布和条件概率分布同样也是高斯分布。

    • Any linear transformation of any 𝑁 jointly Gaussian RV’s results in N RV’s also Gaussian (affine transformation Theorem).

      仿射变换后依然是高斯分布。

  • Ubiquity-Frequently observed 常见的普遍性

    • Central limit theorem (Many distributions we wish to model are truly close to being normal distributions.

      中心极限定理（很多分布可以近似为正态分布\高斯分布）。
